====== Alarms ======

^ Bit ^Description ^
| 0 | Alarm counter 0 |
| 1 | Alarm counter 1 |
| 2 | Alarm counter 2 |
| 3 | Alarm counter 3 |
| 4 | Alarm frequency 0 |
| 5 | Alarm frequency 1 |
| 6 | Alarm frequency 2 |
| 7 | Alarm frequency 3 |









Remember that alarm bits are cleared when read.
